TV Components

Internal Parts of a TV

Learn about the key components inside a modern television.

1. Power Supply Board

Converts AC electricity from the wall into DC power for TV circuits, ensuring stable operation.

2. Main Board (Motherboard)

Processes audio, video, and input signals. Connects to all major parts and manages overall performance.

3. Display Panel

The primary screen where images are displayed using LED, OLED, or QLED technology for stunning visuals.

4. T-Con Board

Controls the timing and signal processing for the display, ensuring smooth image rendering.

5. Backlight System

Provides illumination for the display, typically using LED technology to enhance brightness and contrast.

6. HDMI & Input Ports

Connects external devices like gaming consoles, streaming devices, and cable boxes for media consumption.

How a TV Works

  • Receives and decodes video/audio signals from antennas, cable, or streaming services.
  • Processes images and sound through the mainboard and sends data to the display panel.
  • Backlight illuminates the screen, enhancing image visibility.
  • Sound output is managed by internal speakers or external audio systems.
